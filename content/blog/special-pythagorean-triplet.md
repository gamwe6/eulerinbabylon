<p>A Pythagorean triplet is a set of three natural numbers, <var>a</var> &lt; <var>b</var> &lt; <var>c</var>, for which,</p>
<div style="text-align:center;"> <var>a</var><sup>2</sup> + <var>b</var><sup>2</sup> = <var>c</var><sup>2</sup></div>
<p>For example, 3<sup>2</sup> + 4<sup>2</sup> = 9 + 16 = 25 = 5<sup>2</sup>.</p>
<p>There exists exactly one Pythagorean triplet for which <var>a</var> + <var>b</var> + <var>c</var> = 1000.Find the product <var>abc</var>.</p>
